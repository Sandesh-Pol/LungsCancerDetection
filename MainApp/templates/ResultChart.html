Atelectasis | Standard value : 0.63

Atelectasis is a condition where part or all of a lung collapses. Understanding the importance of deep breathing exercises, coughing, and getting up and moving around after surgery or prolonged bed rest can help prevent atelectasis. Following post-operative care instructions closely is crucial.

Symptoms:

Shortness of breath, rapid breathing, chest pain, coughing.

Care Steps:

Treatment involves deep breathing exercises, coughing exercises, incentive spirometry, and sometimes bronchodilators or suctioning to clear mucus from the airways.
