Finding Lung Cancer Early

Chest X-ray analysis with AI helps spot suspicious areas in the lungs that might be cancerous, potentially leading to earlier detection and treatment.

Learn More

Faster Analysis

By automating some analysis tasks, AI can help doctors review X-rays quicker, potentially improving screening efficiency.

Reduced Radiation

Compared to CT scans, X-rays involve significantly lower radiation exposure, making them ideal for frequent screenings.

Doctor Confirmation Needed

The final diagnosis always comes from a medical professional who will examine the X-ray and consider the AI's findings.

Not a Replacement

While helpful, this technology is not a replacement for doctor's expertise. X-rays may miss some cancers, and AI might flag non-cancerous issues.
